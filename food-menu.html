Burrito

A burrito is a classic Mexican dish consisting of a flour tortilla wrapped around various fillings, typically including seasoned meat (such as beef, chicken, or pork), rice, beans, cheese, and salsa. It's often served with guacamole, sour cream, and additional toppings like lettuce and tomatoes. Burritos are known for their portability and versatility, making them a popular choice for meals on the go or casual dining

Quesadilla

A quesadilla is a simple yet delicious Mexican dish made by filling a tortilla with cheese (and often other ingredients) and then folding it in half and grilling or toasting until the cheese is melted and the tortilla is crispy. Common fillings for quesadillas include shredded chicken, beef, vegetables, and beans. Quesadillas are often served with salsa, guacamole, and sour cream for dipping or topping.

275 calories. Carbs 36.1g Fat 0.2g Protein 0.2g

Taco

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made with a corn or flour tortilla folded or rolled around a filling. The filling can vary widely and may include grilled or seasoned meat (such as beef, chicken, or fish), beans, cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, salsa, and other toppings. Tacos are typically eaten by hand and are known for their bold flavors and customizable nature, allowing each person to create their perfect combination of ingredients.

57 calories. Carbs 15g Fat 0g Protein 0g
